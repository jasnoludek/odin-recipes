Tasty Rhubarb Strawberry Tart

This rhubarb and strawberry tart is the perfect combination of sweet and tangy flavors. The crust is buttery and crunchy, while the filling is creamy and custardy. The tartness of the rhubarb and the sweetness of the strawberries create a perfect balance of flavors. This recipe is easy to make and perfect for a spring or summer dessert.

Ingredients

For the crust.

  • 2 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 package vanilla sugar about 1 tablespoon
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/2 cup ground almonds
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs

For the filling.

  • 1 cup heavy cream 30% fat
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 package vanilla sugar about 1 tablespoon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 package instant vanilla pudding mix

For the topping.

  • 2 cups rhubarb, chopped
  • 2 cups strawberries, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F 175°C. Grease a 9-inch tart pan with cooking spray.
  2. To make the crust, mix together the flour, vanilla sugar, granulated sugar, margarine, ground almonds and egg until a dough forms. Press the dough into the prepared tart
    pan. Use a fork to poke holes in the crust and sprinkle the bread crumbs over the top.
  3. To make the filling, mix together the heavy cream, granulated sugar, vanilla sugar, eggs and instant vanilla pudding mix. Pour the mixture over the crust.
  4. Top the filling with the chopped rhubarb and sliced strawberries.
  5. Bake the tart for about 55 minutes or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown. Let the tart cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
