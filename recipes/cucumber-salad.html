Cucumber Salad

Description

This cucumber salad recipe is perfect for a light and refreshing side dish. The combination of tangy sour cream, fresh
cucumbers, and fragrant herbs creates a delicious and healthy dish that pairs well with grilled meats or fish. It's also easy to
make and perfect for a quick and easy snack.

Ingredients

  • 3 small cucumbers about 1 pound
  • 1 small onion about a half pound
  • fresh dill
  • chives
  • salt
  • pepper
  • half cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Peel cucumbers and cut them into slices. Peel the onion and cut it into small cubes.
  2. In a bowl, mix the sliced cucumbers, the cubed onion, sour cream, chives, salt and pepper
  3. Sprinkle fresh dill on top of the salad
  4. Serve the cucumber salad as a side dish or a refreshing snack. Enjoy!
