Chicken Breasts with Potatoes

Description

This recipe for chicken breasts with potatoes is a light and refreshing summer dish. The chicken breasts are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, while
the potatoes are buttery and delicious. This recipe is easy to make and perfect for a family dinner or a summer barbeque

Ingredients

  • 1 pound early potatoes
  • 1 pound chicken breasts
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and pepper
  • fresh dill

Instructions

  1. Peel the early potatoes and rinse them under cold water. Place them in a pot, cover them with water and bring to a boil. Cook until softened, about 15-20 minutes.
  2. Clean and wash the chicken breasts. In a shallow dish, beat one egg. In another shallow dish, mix together the bread crumbs, salt and pepper. Dip each chicken breast in the
    beaten egg, then coat it in the bread crumbs mixture.
  3. In a large pan, heat the butter over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  4. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them and place them on a serving dish. Sprinkle fresh dill over the top.
  5. Place the cooked chicken breasts on the same serving dish as the potatoes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
